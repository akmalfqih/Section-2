Empowering Indonesia's Capital Market Literacy


Greetings and a warm welcome to KSPM FEB Unila, the Student Capital Market Association at FEB Unila. It is my utmost privilege to serve as the Chairman, and I, Aurora Ariananda Jalita, am excited to embark on this journey with all of you.

At KSPM FEB Unila, our mission is to empower and elevate the literacy of Indonesia's capital market among the masses. We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant community and igniting a profound interest in the captivating world of investments. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the financial industry and pave the way for a brighter economic future.

Join us on this extraordinary voyage, where knowledge knows no bounds, networks flourish, and the mysteries of the capital markets unfold. Together, we have the power to illuminate the path to a prosperous and financially informed society.
With my warmest regards,

Aurora Ariananda Jalita
Chairman of KSPM FEB Unila
